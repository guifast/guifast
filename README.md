## Selected products

### CRM by Fatek

Multi-tenant business management platform combining CRM, ERP, finance, inventory, omnichannel communication and AI-powered automation.

**Stack:** TypeScript, Next.js, React, NestJS, PostgreSQL, Prisma and AWS.

[View product](https://crm.fatek.solutions/)

### Revello

AI-powered vertical ERP for gyms, covering billing, member management, attendance, retention, churn-risk detection and operational AI agents.

**Stack:** TypeScript, Next.js, React, NestJS, PostgreSQL, Prisma and Vitest.

[View product](https://revello.tech/)

### Imob Agents

Vertical SaaS platform that uses AI-powered WhatsApp conversations to update real estate portfolios, structure owner responses and generate prioritized tasks for agents.

**Stack:** TypeScript, Next.js, React, PostgreSQL, Prisma, OpenAI and WhatsApp integrations.

[View product](https://imobagents.com.br/)

### Strong Body Rewards

B2B2C loyalty platform connecting a supplement brand, partner gyms and customers through purchases, validations, levels and rewards.

**Stack:** TypeScript, React, TanStack, Supabase, Cloudflare and Vitest.

[View product](https://club.sbsupplements.com.br/)
